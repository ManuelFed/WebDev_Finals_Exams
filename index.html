TOY STORY

Buzz and Woody soar through the sky in this animated classic that launched a franchise.

Watch Now

Synopsis

Set in a world where toys have a life of their own when people are not present, Toy Story takes moviegoers on a fantastic fun-filled journey, viewed mostly through the eyes of two rival toys - Woody (Tom Hanks), a pull-string talking cowboy, and Buzz Lightyear (Tim Allen), a superhero space action-figure. The comically-mismatched duo eventually learn to put aside their differences when circumstances separate them from their owner, Andy, and they find themselves on a hilarious adventure-filled mission where the only way they can survive is to form an uneasy alliance.

Movie Highlights

Opening Heist Imagination
Andy plays out a bank robbery using his toys, Mr. Potato Head as the villain, Woody as the sheriff, and Slinky Dog as backup. This sets the tone for the toys’ secret lives
Buzz Lightyear’s Arrival
Buzz lands in Andy’s room, believing he’s a real space ranger. His flashy gadgets and charisma quickly make him the new favorite, sparking Woody’s jealousy
“You Are a Toy!” Confrontation
Woody shouts the iconic line at Buzz, forcing him to confront the truth about being a toy. This moment is emotionally pivotal and deeply human
Pizza Planet Adventure
Woody and Buzz sneak into a delivery truck to reach Pizza Planet, leading to chaos in the arcade claw machine where they’re captured by Sid
Sid’s Toy Torture
Sid’s twisted creativity introduces hybrid toys made from broken parts. Woody and Buzz must escape before Sid destroys Buzz with a rocket
Buzz’s Existential Crisis
After seeing a TV ad, Buzz realizes he’s not a real space ranger. He tries to fly and falls, breaking his arm, one of the film’s most heartbreaking scenes
Toy Rebellion and Rescue
Woody rallies Sid’s mutant toys to scare him straight. Buzz regains hope, and they escape just in time to reunite with Andy
The Flying Finale
Woody and Buzz launch into the air using Sid’s rocket, glide into Andy’s car, and finally earn mutual respect and friendship
